We are students from St. Louis University, dedicated to promoting sustainable tourism in the picturesque city of Baguio, Philippines. Nestled amidst breathtaking landscapes, Baguio offers a unique experience that blends natural beauty, rich culture, and warm hospitality. Join us in giving voice to the wonders of Baguio, standing up for its preservation, and making a positive impact on the environment and local communities. Together, we can create a harmonious synergy where travelers and nature thrive hand in hand.
