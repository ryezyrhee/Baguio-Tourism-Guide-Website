GoodTaste Restaurant

Otek St, 2600 Baguio City, Philippines, Baguio

Good Taste Restaurant is a well-known and beloved institution in Baguio City, Philippines. It has been serving a wide range of delicious Filipino and Chinese cuisine for over 50 years, and it’s considered one of the best and busiest restaurants in the city. Because of its popularity, the restaurant is usually full and there’s a long queue of people waiting to dine in, especially during weekends and holidays.
