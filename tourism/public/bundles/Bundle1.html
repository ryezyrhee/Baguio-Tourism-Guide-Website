Travel Plan 1: Cultural and Historical Baguio Tour

Morning

Start your day with a visit to the Baguio Museum and immerse yourself in the rich history and culture of this mountain city. Explore its exhibits, artifacts, and historical treasures to gain a deeper understanding of Baguio's past.

Lunch

Head to Cocina Del Sol, a charming restaurant known for its delicious local and international cuisine. Savor a satisfying meal amidst a cozy atmosphere.

Afternoon

Discover the haunting history of Baguio at the Dominican Old Diplomat Hotel, where you can explore its intriguing corridors and enjoy panoramic views from its hilltop location.

Evening

Wrap up your day with an evening of relaxation at the Baguio Burnham Suites, offering comfortable accommodations and a serene ambiance.
