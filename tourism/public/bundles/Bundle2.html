Travel Plan 2: Culinary Adventure in Baguio

Morning

Start your day with a hearty breakfast at 1896 Bed and Breakfast, known for its delectable morning spread. Fuel up for a day of culinary exploration.

Lunch

Satisfy your appetite with a visit to Craft Burgers by BrrGrr, a haven for burger lovers. Enjoy gourmet burgers with unique flavors and ingredients.

Afternoon

Explore the flavors of Asia with a visit to Kung Jeon Korean Palace. Savor authentic Korean cuisine that will tantalize your taste buds

Evening

Conclude your food journey with a fun-filled session of bowling or billiards. Challenge your travel companions to some friendly competition.
