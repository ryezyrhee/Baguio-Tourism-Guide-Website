Travel Plan 3: Adventure and Nature Escape

Morning

Embark on an adventure at Tree Top Adventures, where you can enjoy thrilling ziplines and treetop adventures, surrounded by the lush beauty of Baguio's natural landscape.

Lunch

Recharge at Good Tastee, a local favorite for affordable and delicious Filipino dishes. Sample some local flavors and specialties.

Afternoon

Hike to the breathtaking Mt. Yangbew, a picturesque mountain known for its stunning views of the Cordillera mountain range. Capture unforgettable moments amidst nature's beauty.

Evening

Retreat to the comfort of A Hotel Baguio, where you can relax and unwind after an exhilarating day of adventure.
