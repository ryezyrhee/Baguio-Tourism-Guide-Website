Mount Yangbew

Tawang, La Trinidad, Luzon 2601, Philippines


Are you ready for an unforgettable mountain adventure in the heart of Benguet?

          The trail to the summit is perfect for novice hikers, starting with a paved concrete road and then transitioning to a dusty terrain as you ascend. But the adventure doesn't end with hiking alone. At Mt. Yangbew, you can also enjoy biking and horseback riding, adding layers of excitement to your visit. There are cozy huts at the summit, providing the perfect spot to rest, savor the views, and take in the tranquility of this natural wonder. Join us for an unforgettable experience at Mt. Yangbew and let the beauty of Benguet's 'Little Pulag' take your breath away.
