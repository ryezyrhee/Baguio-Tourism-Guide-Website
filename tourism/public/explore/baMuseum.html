Baguio Museum

Baguio Museum, Dot-PTA Complex, Gov. Pack Rd, Baguio, 2600 Benguet


          Discover Baguio City's history and culture at the small yet captivating Baguio Museum. This must-visit destination offers an enriching experience for history and museum enthusiasts. Learn about the city's inception, development, and significant events, including American involvement and World War II. The museum's unique architecture, under renovation, blends modern and traditional design. Affordable admission fees make it accessible to all, and you can explore indigenous materials, dioramas, sculptures, and even a mummified corpse. The second level houses historical photographs, and there's potential for future exhibits. Don't miss visiting Ernesto Dap-Ay's booth. Support this vital establishment as it preserves Baguio's heritage.
