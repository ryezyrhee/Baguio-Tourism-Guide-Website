Diplomat Hotel

Heritage Hill and Nature Park Garden (Old Diplomat Hotel), Dominican Hill, Diplomat Road, Baguio, 2600 Benguet


           Explore the Old Diplomat Hotel in Baguio City, a captivating destination with a rich history and a touch of mystery. Visit during the day to enjoy the unique atmosphere and take photos. Nearby, a small park offers a peaceful retreat. You can also embark on a scenic walk from Baguio City to the hotel and the Our Lady of Lourdes Grotto. While the hotel shows signs of aging, its hilltop location provides stunning views of Baguio City, especially from the roof. The hotel's second floor has an eerie ambiance, and some visitors have reported unusual temperature shifts. Known for its haunted stories, the Old Diplomat Hotel is a must-visit historical landmark with a tragic past, but note that staying past 5 PM is not allowed. Discover the hotel's enigmatic charm and its place in Baguio's history when you visit.
