1896 Bed & Breakfast

1896 Bed and Breakfast, CH3V+QRG, Kisad Rd, Baguio, Benguet


Welcome to the 1896 Hotel and Breakfast, a unique gem nestled in the heart of Baguio City. This charming establishment is housed within a historic wooden and concrete structure, reminiscent of a bygone era. If you're seeking an authentic experience rather than a cookie-cutter hotel, this is the place for you. While it may not boast brand-new facilities, its character and warmth more than make up for it.
